Abstract

We focused on the XOR gate as an operator for molecular programing.
The basic operators that AND, OR, and NOTgate have already been realized in the DNA reacting system,
so the XORgate can be realized to combine with these operators in theory.
However, the combinational circuit is very complex, because per XORgate needs two ANDgates, an ORgates, and two NOTgate.
For this reason, we launched the design of XORgate that has simple reacting system without the basic operators.
The XORgate designed in this time has two advantages.
First, It is easy to design, and the number of DNA strands on the XORgate designed is less than half one on the XOR gate of the combination circuit.
Second, The XORgate designed operates faster than the XORgate of the combination circuit. The XORgate designed functioned alone, so the number of strand displacement reactions reduced, and we succeeded in making fast the response time.
In this way, the XORgate designed in this time is superior to the XORgate of the combination circuit in terms of cost and performance.
We named the XOR gate designed in this time “DNA Conductor” after?.
