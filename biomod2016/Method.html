4.Output confirmation
        We applied FRET to confirm how much output was released. Before the output reacted with Fluorescence, the FAM did not fluoresce since the fluorescence of FAM was absorbed by BHQ1. When output reacted with Fluorescence, the fluorescence of FAM became detectable because the distance between the FAM and BHQ1 molecules increased (Figure4-1).
